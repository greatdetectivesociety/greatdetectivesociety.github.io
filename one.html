IDENTIFICATION CARD
Mackenzie / Rina Ozawa (小沢里奈)
Role: Protaganist
Job: Aspiring Detective

She follows her family's business as a detective, but has trouble finding cases. After solving four cases, she joins the Great Detective Society and gains the title "Touch Detective".

TOWN CITIZEN OF OSAWARI
Penelope / Manami (まなみ)
Role: Mackenzie's Friend
Job: N/A

She is one of the girls that lives in Beatrice's Condominium (Room 102) in town. She's rather air-headed and forgetful, and gets caught up in incidents all the time.

TOWN CITIZEN OF OSAWARI
Chloe / Chitose (ちとせ)
Role: Mackenzie's Friend or Rival
Job: N/A

She calls herself "Chloe the Super Sleuth". She always appears to help Mackenzie on a case. She is daring and generally doesn't think about consequences, which results on her getting into trouble.

TOWN CITIZEN OF OSAWARI
Cromwell / Jii (じい)
Role: Mackenzie's Butler
Job: Butler / Inventor

He is a butler that serves Mackenzie's family for several years. He is a genius inventor and uses his inventions and knowledge to help Mackenzie with her cases.

TOWN CITIZEN OF OSAWARI
Funghi / Nameko (なめこ)
Role: Mackenzie's Friend
Job: Detective's Assistent

Funghi is an assistant sent from the Great Detective Society and can be used to solve puzzles. All it can say is "nnf .... nnf ..."

TOWN CITIZEN OF OSAWARI
Daisy / Deijī (デイジー)
Role: Town Citizen
Job: Naked Bones Employee

She lives in room 203 of Beatrice's Condominium, since moving in she's been angry with Penelope's constant noise in the room below. She has a part-time job in the clothing boutique Naked Bones.

TOWN CITIZEN OF OSAWARI
Antoinette / Praline (プラリーネ Purarīne)
Role: Town Citizen
Job: Sweet Mountain Owner

She is the owner of the pastry shop Sweet Mountain, her main client being Dover. She's always trying new pastries and cakes with strange ingredients.

TOWN CITIZEN OF OSAWARI
Dover / Buraddo (ブラッド)
Role: Town Citizen
Job: Former Circus Performer

He is an anthropomorphic shark that is always at Antoinette's Sweet Mountain eating all types of sweets. He used to work on the Circus by performing with the tattoos in his back, moving them with his muscles.

TOWN CITIZEN OF OSAWARI
Katrina / Katorīnu Ayaka (カトリーヌ・綾香)
Role: Town Citizen
Job: Fortune Teller

She is the fortune teller at the House of Illusions. She gives advice for Mackenzie, but it is usually said in a manner that she has difficulty understanding.

TOWN CITIZEN OF OSAWARI
Beatrice / Yoneko (ヨネ子)
Role: Town Citizen
Job: Landlord

She is the landlord of the girls-only Condominium where Penelope lives. She enjoys watching TV all day long and eating bonbons.

TOWN CITIZEN OF OSAWARI
Myron and Myrtle /
Alumel (アルメル) and Chromel (クロメル)
Role: Town Citizen
Job: Security Guards

They are two acquaintances of Katrina that guard the door of her fortune-telling house in the first game. However, in the second game they work as private security guards at the Department Store.

TOWN CITIZEN OF OSAWARI
Lugol / Rugōru (ルゴール)
Role: Organ Grinder
Job: Circus Performer

He is an organ grinder from Touch Detective that is usually performing in front of the Planetarium. He rarely speaks, usually ignoring anyone who talks with him and talking only once in the story.

OSAWARI TOWN VISITOR
The Circus Master (団長)
Role: Circus Master
Job: Circus Performer

The circus ringmaster. A forgetful, sleepy, troublesome person. Despite those traits, he is an amazing performer and wild animal trainer, being able to tame the beast Karbanog.

OSAWARI TOWN VISITOR
Karbanog (カルバノグ)
Role: Vicious Beast
Job: Circus Performer

Karbanog is the most vicious animal in the world, having sharp fangs, moving like the wind, and everywhere it goes, a trail of blood follows. The Circus Master is the only person who can control it.

OSAWARI TOWN VISITOR
Barnaby / Umizō (ウミゾー)
Role: Town Citizen
Job: Ice Skating Rink Owner

He is the owner of the Ice Skating Rink, but he wants to turn it into a pool. I wonder how they manage to run the rink when they hate the cold?

TOWN CITIZEN OF OSAWARI
Ogden (コペル)
Role: Penelope's Kidnapper
Job: Planetarium Director

Ogden is a serious astronomer and the director of the Planetarium. He loves too see the stars in the Planetarium.

OSAWARI TOWN VISITOR
Lynsey / Mira (ミラ)
Role: Planetarium Attendant
Job: N/A

She is a small girl that likes stars, dolls and chocolate, often visits the Planetarium to see the stars and get the free chocolate. She is always carrying a hand puppet named Lini (マギ, Magi).
