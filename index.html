The Great Detective Society is a fan-site for Touch Detective, this site aims to store and archive all things related to touch detective, started on December 4th, 2023.

But what is touch detective?
Touch Detective is a point and click trilogy game focusing on adventure and mystery, it is also known as Mystery Detective and Osawari Tantei: Ozawa Rina (おさわり探偵 小沢里奈)
