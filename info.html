Touch Detective
Touch Detective is a point and click trilogy game focusing on adventure and mystery, it is also known as Mystery Detective and Osawari Tantei: Ozawa Rina (おさわり探偵 小沢里奈). It's where you play as a aspiring detective, Mackenzie as she solves the mysteries in her town.
